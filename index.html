Our History.

Myself (Danai) and George were inspired by the evolution of marketing and advertising in the commercial world. This saw the birth of SocialBook, we saw the need for interactive advertising and marketing that fits with the change that has happened in the world. Newspapers were paper in the far past, online websites in the past and now news is passed through social platforms. Marketind and advertising have changed. People would watch recorded movies in the far past, live TV in the past and now Facebook shows live videos. SocialBook was then established in January 2016 based in Zimbabwe.
